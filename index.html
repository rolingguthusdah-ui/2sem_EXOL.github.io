EXO (엑소) is a 9-member South Korean boy group that currently consists of: Suho, Xiumin, Lay, Baekhyun, Chen, Chanyeol, D.O., Kai, and Sehun. On April 8, 2022, Lay announced his departure from SM Ent., no details regarding his possible future activities as an EXO member have been given, however, as of 2025 he has been removed as a member of EXO on SM's official website.

Despite being removed from the official website, Lay did participating in EXO's upcoming comeback, REVERXE.
